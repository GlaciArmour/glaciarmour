About GlaciArmour

GlaciArmour's mission is to help you make time for the people you love while taking care of the car you love.

GlaciArmour is run by a group of high school students who aim to help people save their time and energy on removing snow off of their car(s).

Our Product

Don't have time to scrape a surprise snowstorm off of your car?

GlaciArmour helps you reduce time and energy spent on cleaning snow off your windshield by providing you with a convenient alternative to a regular snow brush. With convenience at hand, you can use our windshield protector (GlaciArmour) to protect against ice pellets, snow and hail. Once you are ready to go, simply detach the easy-to-secure Velcro on the windshield protector, and snow will fall off with it! All it takes are two easy steps: Put it over your windshield when you park your car and take it off when you're ready to drive. With convenience and ease, GlaciArmour helps you save time and effort in your day-to-day life, giving yourself more time to spend on the things you love every day.
